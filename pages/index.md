This archive has been created by Avery Shipp to act as the digital backbone of a small community driven archive. This archive will take the physical form of a little free library, designed to give the community access to its own history, while also giving community members a way to interact and contribute their own stories. 



What we want to be:
- Ethical
- Collaborative
- Engaging
- Truthful

How does this archive work? 

That's up to you!
This archive is designed to be community based, easily accessible, and straightforward to use. 

If you are just here to explore and learn more, go check out the collections tab! Click through the various digital artifacts that we have or filter through them using their tags (such as location, time period, or artifact type). Every artifact in the collection is contributed by someone in the community in a way that allows them to maintain ownership over the history of the artifact and to tell the story the way that they believe it needs to be told. This may result in conflicting artifacts, stories, or perspectives- that's ok! This archive is designed to collect individual stories told by the individual, that means that there will be discrepencies, but that doesn't discredit the story that is being told. 

Do you feel like you have a story that needs to be told?
Great! We are always excited to add new histories to the archive. 

Additions can happen in a few different ways. The easiest is by filling out the contribution form, which will ask you a variety of questions designed to collect the necessary archival information on your artifact. Some of the questions will be more focused on the background information necessary for us to store data, such as the format of the item that is being contributed. Other questions will be how you want to tell the story.
